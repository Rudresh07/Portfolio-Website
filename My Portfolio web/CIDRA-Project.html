CIDRA

The project aimed to address the inefficiencies in conventional methods of disseminating academic information within the college community. Faced with challenges in managing and accessing crucial data through existing platforms like WhatsApp groups, the team embarked on developing a comprehensive Student Information System (SIS). The SIS comprised frontend, backend, and Android app components to provide a holistic solution.

Problem Statement

Problem: Inefficiencies in disseminating academic information within the college community through conventional means like WhatsApp groups and messages posed challenges in managing and accessing crucial data.

Solution

To overcome these challenges, our multidisciplinary team collaborated on developing a comprehensive Student Information System (SIS). This encompassed frontend, backend, and Android app components. The Android app, developed under my lead, played a crucial role. Built with Kotlin and XML, it provided students with seamless access to lectures, assignments, and a problem submission system. Simultaneously, a website was developed for teachers and administrators, creating a holistic and user-friendly solution that streamlined academic processes and communication..

Role

As an Android developer on the project, my role involved leading the development of the Android app. I focused on creating a user-centric interface, facilitating efficient access to academic resources and enhancing overall communication within the college community..
